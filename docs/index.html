Hi, I'm Charlie

I'm a creative developer based in New York City. I love operating where development and design intersect. That - paired with the puzzle and power of design at scale - has lead me to my current work with design systems! Check out some of my recent work below.

Spotify Encore

While on contract with the Encore Design Systems team at Spotify, I've been fortunate to participate in initiatives ranging from accessibility, to a typescale migration and forward compatibility. Featured here is my article on Customization vs Configuration in Design Systems

Publicis Marcel

Publicis Marcel is an internal tool for Publicis Groupe, harnessing their global workforce through the 'Power of One'. I had the pleasure of helping build Marcel's design system from the ground up, and helping it scale to accomodate 5+ feature teams around the globe.

Trans Rochester Speaks

A digital exhibit on the history of trans people in Rochester, NY. Oral histories, photography, and resources for the trans community and allies are showcased within this webapp built in sass and handlebars.
