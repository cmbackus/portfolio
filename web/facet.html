Facet

Our team created an interactive display case which brings the context and detail provided in online shopping into the in store retail experience. This web app provides in depth information about the product, as well as the brand's history, icons, timeline, and social media. This project took place over one semester, with the final exhibit displayed at Rochester Institute of Technology's Imagine R.I.T festival
